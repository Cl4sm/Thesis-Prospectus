## What to Learn

### Structure Patterns
- How much does the introduction differ between proposal and final dissertation?
- How are published papers adapted into chapters?
- How long are related work sections relative to the whole?
- How is the proposed work chapter structured in the final version (after the work is done)?

### Connor's Template
`wiki/reference_material/dissertation/connor_2024/template.tex` contains a LaTeX template you might compare against your current setup.

### Dissertation → Proposal Mapping
Compare Kyle's `proposal.pdf` with his `dissertation.pdf` to see exactly how the proposal condensed from the full version. This is the most direct model for your 20-page compression task.
