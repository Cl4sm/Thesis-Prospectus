**Citation:** Cheng et al., 2018
**Role in your thesis:** Comparison target in [[Chapter 2 - Related Work]]

Detects taint-style vulnerabilities in embedded device firmware but only handles individual binaries. Cannot reason about multi-binary data flows (the cross-binary interactions that [[Environment Resolution]] in [[Operation Mango]] catches).
