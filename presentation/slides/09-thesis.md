Here is the thesis. Accurate, complete, and scalable autonomous vulnerability discovery is achievable by building systems that progressively close the gap between detection and confirmation. [pause] The key insight is that we do not need perfect evidence from the start. We need systems that begin with what is scalable, then add richer evidence as we move through the analysis. Detection identifies candidates completely. Validation filters them accurately through multiple independent techniques. Confirmation grounds the final report in concrete, executable input. Each step narrows the candidate set and deepens the evidence. Together, they close the gap.
