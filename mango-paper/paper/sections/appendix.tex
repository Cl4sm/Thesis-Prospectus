\subsection{Additional Firmware Tests}
To show our extensibility beyond the \karonteDataset, we randomly selected 6 firmware samples from different vendors (Shown in Table~\ref{tab:appendix_firmware_test}).
There was no additional effort or analyses added to \toolname to generate these results; they were produced in the same manner as the results discussed in table~\ref{tab:mango_satc} and table~\ref{tab:mango_satc_manual}.
\toolname found a significant number of \trupoc's in these firmware samples, including \trupoc's in Belkin and Linksys firmwares which were not tested at all in the \karonteDataset.
Each generated \trupoc was manually analyzed to determine the overall TP rate, which was 41.3\% %.
